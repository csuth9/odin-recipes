Seasoned Rice

Description

Simple and quick. A rice meal that has been seasoned with amazing herbs and spices. A savory, buttery, herb-infused side dish that pairs nicely with almost everything and can be eaten on its own. It's the ideal comfort food that the entire family will love! Furthermore, it costs less and only needs a small amount of effort to manufacture. Your new go-to rice recipe will undoubtedly be this seasoned rice in no time!

In addition to being delicious and addictive, it is also quite simple to create. Since it is a one-pot meal, all you have to do is throw everything in the pot and wait for it to cook, it won't take up much of your time.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Parsley
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a large pot and give it a stir.
  2. Place on the stove and bring to a boil over high heat.
  3. Turn the heat to low. As soon as it starts to bubble, cover and reduce to low heat.
  4. Let it simmer. Simmer for about 18-20 minutes or until all the liquid is fully absorbed.
  5. Turn off the heat. Let it rest for about 5 minutes, add another tablespoon of butter, if desired, then fluff with a fork and serve.
